Common Questions

We've heard every
concern. Here's the truth.

What if I want to sell the property?

The ChargePark agreement transfers to the new owner automatically. In fact, it adds value — buyers receive a built-in revenue stream and modern EV infrastructure from day one.

What does "zero investment" actually mean?

ChargePark covers 100% of hardware costs, electrical upgrades, permitting, installation, and ongoing maintenance. You pay absolutely nothing upfront or over the term.

Who handles maintenance and repairs?

We do. Our team handles all hardware maintenance, software updates, and replacements. If a charger goes down, we fix it — at our expense, on our schedule.

What if tenant EV adoption is low right now?

EV adoption among renters is growing fast, especially in quality multi-family housing. ChargePark becomes a competitive amenity that attracts higher-income, EV-driving tenants.

How does billing and payment work?

Tenants pay ChargePark directly via app or card. We compile monthly revenue, calculate your 50% share, and deposit directly to your account with a full report.

What types of properties qualify?

We work with apartment complexes, condominiums, mixed-use buildings, and HOA communities with private parking. Minimum 20 units. Contact us to confirm your property qualifies.
